# Company Handbook (excerpt)

## Working hours
Standard hours are 09:00-17:30 Central European Time. Flexible start within a
two-hour window is permitted with manager approval.

## Password rotation policy
Passwords for production systems must be rotated every 90 days. SSO accounts
follow the identity provider's policy (currently 180 days). Service accounts
use long-lived tokens stored in the company secrets vault.

## Q3 finance summary
Revenue grew 11% year-over-year. Operating margin was 17%. Headcount increased
by 8 across engineering and product.
